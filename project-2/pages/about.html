About Me

Hi! My name is Lauren Godwin. I chose to do my Instructable on cheesecake bites because cheesecake is my all time favorite dessert.
The only problem with a normal cheesecake is the time it takes to make it. That's why these bites are the perfect alternative!

I have made these delicious treats for many occasions and they always turn out as a huge hit!

The original recipe calls for cherry pie filling but in my personal opinion that is not my favorite. Being from Montana my go to topping for these cheesecakes are Huckleberries!
You can boil 2 cups of frozen (or thawed) Huckleberries with 1/3 cup of sugar, once boiled, add 2 Tbls of cornstarch and 1.5 tsps of water. Simmer for 5-8 min then stir until thickened.
And there you go...the perfect Huckleberry sauce. Mmmmm, doesn't that sound amazing?!
