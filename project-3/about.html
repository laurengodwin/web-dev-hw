Elsa Godwin

Elsa Godwin was born in Missoula, MT. Godwin started her volleyball career in 5th grade at Montana Volleyball Academy. She is currently a senior at Sentinel High school but will graduate in May of 2018.
She will then attend the University of Montana, majoring in Business and Teaching. She has a full ride scholarship through the Griz Volleyball program.
Godwin is currently ranked 2nd in Montana for volleyball. She is a 5'10" outside hitter.
Over her high school career, Godwin has had 287 kills, 17 blocks, 192 digs, and 40 aces.

Godwin loves to volunteer at Missoula Alliance Church to teach little kids. She also enjoys various social events including going to other Sentinel sport games.
Godwin is a born leader and shows it on and off the court. She has a 4.0 GPA, and will graduate with multiple AP and dual credit classes.
She is strong-willed and confident. Her biggest characteristics are being humble and loving to anyone she encounters.
I am very lucky to call this beautiful girl my sister.
