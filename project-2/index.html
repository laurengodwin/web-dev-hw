How to make Cheesecake Bites

What You Need

  • 12 vanilla wafers
  • cupcake liners
  • 2 pkg. (8oz. each) Cream Cheese, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup cherry pie filling, optional

Make It

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Place 1 wafer in each 12 paper-lined muffin cups.
  3. Beat cream cheese and sugar in small bowl with mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing after each addition just until blended.
  4. Put cheesecake batter overtop wafers with spoon in cupcake liners.
  5. Bake 30 min. Turn off oven; open over door slightly. Let stand in oven 30 min. Remove from oven; cool completely.
  6. Top with pie filling (optional).
  7. Refrigerate 1 hour.
